About

I'm Berta (bear + water in british english minus the 'wa')

I was born in Barcelona, spent the last years living in The Netherlands, Sweden, Ireland and the UK until I finally settled in Madrid, Spain - looking for some sunshine, I guess!


Making a difference through fun, impactful, and conscious design.

My aim is to create experiences that improve users' lives, whether making their day-to-day easier, helping them access the inaccessible or just having some necessary fun. Before becoming a product designer, I spent several years selling software: low-code apps, CRM, eCommerce platforms (you name it!), but ended up pursuing what I’m most passionate about: design. This combination of experiences allows me to have a holistic design approach, where I build human-centered products, without giving up on business needs. Being design and business savy is uncommon in the design world, which puts me in a great position to add unique value to projects. Contrarily to many, I don't aim to bridge the gap between humans and technology. In fact, I quite like that gap. My objective lays on the improvement of humans’ lives through digital products, without it meaning a complete dependance on them. This is why I focus on designing impactful, valuable and ethical products. Aside of building user flows and wireframes, I'm a hardcore music addict, avid traveler, conversation squeezer, and a massive lover of bread in all its shapes and forms.
